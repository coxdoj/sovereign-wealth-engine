# A Prayer for Healing, Cleansing, and Preservation

Father, in the mighty name of Jesus Christ,
this Wednesday I come boldly
to the throne of grace,
to obtain mercy, favor,
and forgiveness for my sins.

By the blood of Jesus,
I decree restoration and preservation over my body.
Let Your Holy Spirit breathe upon me
and make every part fully functional,
according to Your divine design.

For it is written:

> “The LORD is righteous;
> He loves justice;
> the upright will see His face.”
> — Psalms 11:7

And again:

> “Then Elisha said, ‘Get some flour.’
> He put it into the pot and said,
> ‘Serve it to the people to eat.’
> And there was nothing harmful in the pot.”
> — 2 Kings 4:41

O God of Elisha,
You whose faithfulness does not change,
let every area of my life
receive Zoe life from Mount Zion.

Holy Ghost,
search me completely.
Remove and evict everything
that stands below Your divine purpose for me.

I decree healing
by the power of the Holy Ghost.

Every arrow of darkness,
every hidden contamination,
every affliction not planted by God—
let it meet the standard of heaven’s protection.

I speak the name of Jesus Christ,
whose blood speaks a better word
than the blood of Abel.

I rest in Your faithfulness,
dear Yahweh.

All glory returns to You alone.
Let me decrease
that Your incorruptible Spirit,
El Elyon,
may be revealed.

Every death in the pot,
every trap against my destiny,
meets the fire and cleansing power
of the Holy Ghost.

And now I decree:

Let my body be filled
with the healing life of Jesus Christ.

---

Amen.
