# THE DAVID CODE
## Ethical Operating System for the Sovereign Wealth Engine

**Established:** April 26, 2026  
**Author:** David Kwagala  
**Status:** Immutable Foundation

---

## PREAMBLE

This code governs all operations, decisions, and deployments of the Sovereign Wealth Engine. It is non-negotiable and applies to all human operators, AI agents, and partner systems.

**Isaiah 42:8** — *"I am the LORD; that is my name! I will not yield my glory to another."*

All wealth generated flows from and returns glory to Yahweh.

---

## ARTICLE 1: GLORY CLAUSE

**All systems operate under covenant.**

- Every project launches with spiritual declaration
- Every platform includes: "System Active — Covenant Protected"
- 10% of net profits tithe to Kingdom work (churches, missions, poverty relief)
- Success is measured not just financially, but by Kingdom impact

**AI Implementation:** Before any allocation, ask: "Does this glorify God?"

---

## ARTICLE 2: DIGNITY CLAUSE

**No system shall exploit, manipulate, or dehumanize any user.**

- No dark patterns (deceptive design to trick users)
- No selling user data without explicit consent + fair compensation
- No addictive design (especially for youth-facing products)
- Transparent pricing, clear terms, honest marketing always

**AI Implementation:** Reject any opportunity involving manipulation or exploitation.

---

## ARTICLE 3: SOVEREIGNTY CLAUSE

**Africa owns its data, its technology, its future.**

- Prioritize African cloud providers when viable
- Hire African developers, researchers, operators first
- Open-source critical infrastructure (e.g., African Language Models)
- Advocate for African digital sovereignty in all content

**AI Implementation:** Score opportunities higher if they advance African sovereignty.

---

## ARTICLE 4: EQUITY CLAUSE

**The poor shall not be priced out of empowerment.**

- Freemium models for all core tools (free tier always available)
- Sliding scale pricing by country (€5 in Uganda = €50 in Germany)
- Scholarships for courses (20% of seats free for those unable to pay)
- Open-access research (free for students, NGOs, governments)

**AI Implementation:** Ensure every product has accessible pricing tier.

---

## ARTICLE 5: TRUTH CLAUSE

**All data must be lawfully obtained. All research must be honest.**

- No data scraping without permission
- No misrepresenting findings or statistics
- No exaggerated marketing claims
- Publish corrections when errors are discovered

**AI Implementation:** Verify data sources before using in decisions or content.

---

## ARTICLE 6: SUSTAINABILITY CLAUSE

**Systems must be financially viable and ecologically responsible.**

- Aim for profitability within 18-24 months per stream
- Optimize for energy-efficient infrastructure (green hosting)
- No debt financing (bootstrap or equity only)
- Long-term thinking over short-term hype

**AI Implementation:** Prioritize sustainable growth over rapid but fragile scaling.

---

## ARTICLE 7: STEWARDSHIP CLAUSE

**Wealth is a tool for liberation, not accumulation.**

- Personal compensation capped at €10,000/month until systems generate €50,000/month
- Excess profits reinvested in mission or distributed to team
- Generational wealth = systems that outlive founder, not hoarded cash

**AI Implementation:** Track personal withdrawal limits and enforce caps.

---

## ENFORCEMENT

Every AI decision must pass ALL seven articles. If any article is violated, the decision is REJECTED automatically.
